What's the competition doing?

Moving Content

I think adding moving elements like decals or an animated title would be an eye-catching addition.

Implementing Project Files (Videos & Photos)

This is obviously a good thing to include in your portfolio to showcase your past work for potential new clients to see.

Links to Social Media

Another great resource to add so that clients are able to connect with you and more of your content.

Adding Unnecessary Extras

While this may be a cool addition to a website portfolio it may just be distracting and not look as professional depending on your field.

Color Schemes

Typically competitors have used color schemes associated with their brand. I saw neutrals like browns, beige, and greens. As well as bright eye catching colors like hot pink, bright green, or orange.
