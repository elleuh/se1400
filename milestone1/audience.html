My Audience

My targeted audience would be businesses looking for help with their social media.

Geolocation

While you can do social media freelance for people everywhere, I would probably focus on companies in my general area (Greater St. George Area).

Age

I would say the typical age for my specific audience would be clients ranging from 30-65.

Gender

In St.George I feel that most businesses are run by men, therefore my target audeince would mainly be men. However there are several women owned companies as well so it would ultimately be both!

Other

My typical audience may also be associated with a new business and established businesses with not much social media outreach.
